## Problem Description
The customer inquired about several features and compliance aspects of the Netwrix Endpoint Protector (EPP) during a demo session, specifically regarding language support, compliance with the DPDP Act, compatibility with IceWarp email client, and file labeling capabilities.

## Environment Details
- The customer is using the IceWarp email client with an on-premises email server.

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Reviewed the language support capabilities of the Netwrix EPP, specifically for Hindi, Marathi, and Gujarati.
2. Assessed compliance with the DPDP Act.
3. Evaluated compatibility of Netwrix EPP with the IceWarp email client.
4. Investigated the functionality for labeling files as confidential and restricting sharing and copying.

## Root Cause
The Netwrix EPP does not support compliance with the DPDP Act, which was a key concern raised by the customer.

## Solution
The resolution involved clarifying that:
- The Netwrix EPP does not support the DPDP Act.
- Language support for Hindi, Marathi, and Gujarati in the custom content deny list feature is not available.
- The solution is compatible with the IceWarp email client.
- Files can be labeled as confidential, and restrictions can be applied to sharing and copying.

## Notes
- It is important to communicate clearly about compliance limitations, such as the lack of support for the DPDP Act, to avoid misunderstandings in future inquiries.
- Ensure to verify language support capabilities before demo sessions to provide accurate information to customers.
