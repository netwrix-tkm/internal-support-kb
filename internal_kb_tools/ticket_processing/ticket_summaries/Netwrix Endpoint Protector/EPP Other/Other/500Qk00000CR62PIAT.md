## Problem Description
The customer reported that after successfully applying a recent hotfix to their DLP console, the hotfix still appeared as available for update in the live update section.

## Environment Details
- Product: Netwrix Endpoint Protector
- Component: DLP Console

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. Customer confirmed the hotfix was applied successfully.
2. Customer was advised to check the "View applied updates" section to verify if the hotfix was listed.
3. Technical support acknowledged the issue as a known problem and informed the customer that it was being addressed by the DevOps team.

## Root Cause
The issue was identified as a known problem where the DLP console incorrectly displays the hotfix as available for update even after it has been applied.

## Solution
The customer was informed that the issue was being worked on by the DevOps team and that the fix was expected to be released within the following week. In the meantime, the customer was advised to disregard the hotfix notification if it appeared in the applied updates list.

## Notes
- Customers experiencing similar issues should check the "View applied updates" section to confirm if the hotfix is listed as applied.
- If the hotfix appears in the update list despite being applied, it can be disregarded until a permanent fix is implemented by the DevOps team.
