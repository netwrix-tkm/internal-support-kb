## Problem Description
The customer reported that a new RFID reader was marked as blocked in the device control system, despite the device functioning correctly. This indicated a potential glitch in the interpretation of the device's status.

## Environment Details
- **Device Type:** RFID Reader
- **Platform:** Netwrix Endpoint Protector

## Troubleshooting Steps
1. The customer confirmed that the RFID reader was operational despite being marked as blocked.
2. The support team communicated with the customer to gather more details about the issue.
3. A follow-up was conducted to check if the issue persisted or if further assistance was needed.

## Root Cause
The root cause of the issue was identified as a glitch in the device control system that incorrectly interpreted the status of the RFID reader, leading to it being marked as blocked despite its functionality.

## Solution
The issue was resolved after a discussion between the support engineer, Andrei Pop, and the customer. The customer confirmed that the device was functioning correctly and requested to close the ticket, indicating that the problem was no longer a concern.

## Notes
- Ensure to monitor device status updates in the Netwrix Endpoint Protector to prevent similar glitches in the future.
- It may be beneficial to implement additional checks or alerts for devices that are marked as blocked but are still operational to avoid confusion for users.
